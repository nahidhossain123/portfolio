About

WHO AM I ?

Design is an art of imagination & the Fashion is an Entertainment.

The name is Mohammad Tariqual Islam. I have been working in fashion industry more than two years. I am a continuous seeker of knowledge.

From university life, I always dreamed that one day I would be puppeteer of design & the design would be my puppets. I worked on different of fashion like couture, 3D, origami, fashion art, TR cut, sustainable, zero-waste design, sustainable dye, ready to wear, ornamentation, Avant grade, fusion fashion etc. I also performed at national & international platform on design. I did not come easily. I have gone through lots of barrier & complicated situation. However, my dream is still alive. I am a person who is positive about every aspect of life.

End of the day I can smile a little bit for my confident on work of design that I achieved.
